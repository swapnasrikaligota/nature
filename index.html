About Nature:

Nature encompasses everything in the physical world that isn’t created by humans. This includes living organisms, landscapes, weather, and celestial bodies. The term “nature” comes from the Latin word natura, meaning “birth” or "essential qualities"1. Nature is vital to our lives in many ways: Biodiversity: It supports a wide variety of life forms, from plants and animals to microorganisms. Ecosystem Services: Nature provides essential services like clean air and water, pollination of crops, and climate regulation.
